Career Roadmap — UI/UX Design

Your pathway from internship graduate to senior design leader. Roles, salaries, certifications, milestones, and a 90-day job-search plan.

Career Progression

🌱

Junior UI Designer

0–1 year ₹3–6 LPA
  • Figma fundamentals
  • Wireframing and low-fi mockups
  • Basic user research participation
  • Visual design principles
  • Following existing style guides
⚡

UI/UX Designer

1–3 years ₹6–14 LPA
  • End-to-end product design
  • Conducting usability tests
  • Design systems contribution
  • Stakeholder presentations
  • Advanced Figma (variants, prototyping)
🔧

Product Designer

2–5 years ₹12–25 LPA
  • Design strategy & vision
  • Metrics-driven design decisions
  • Cross-functional collaboration
  • Research leadership
  • Design system ownership (partial)
🚀

Senior Product Designer

4–7 years ₹22–45 LPA
  • Design system ownership
  • Research ops & program management
  • Mentoring junior designers
  • Design critique culture
  • C-suite design advocacy
☁️

UX Researcher

1–5 years ₹8–25 LPA
  • Qual & quant research methods
  • Moderated usability studies
  • Insights synthesis & reporting
  • Research ops infrastructure
  • Stakeholder communication
💼

Head of Design / Design Director

7+ years ₹40–80+ LPA
  • Design org leadership & hiring
  • Design strategy at company level
  • C-suite alignment & reporting
  • Team culture & career development
  • Cross-product design consistency

Certifications Worth Getting

FREE

Google UX Design Certificate (Coursera)

~₹3,000/month subscription

7-course programme covering the full UX design process from research to prototyping. Portfolio-ready projects included. Widely recognised by Indian tech companies as a credible entry-level credential.

AFFORDABLE

Interaction Design Foundation (IxDF)

~₹12,000/year

Deep-dive courses on all UX topics — user research, design thinking, mobile UX, accessibility. Includes mentor sessions and a certificate per course. Best ongoing learning investment for serious UX professionals.

PREMIUM

Nielsen Norman Group UX Certification

~$1,000 (study materials + exam)

The highest UX credential in the industry. Requires completing a series of NN/g courses and passing an exam. Essential for senior UX roles at top-tier companies. Demonstrates deep, research-backed expertise.

AFFORDABLE

Adobe XD Certified Professional

~₹8,000 exam fee

Adobe's official certification for XD proficiency. Strong signal for enterprise and agency roles that use Adobe tools. Validates prototyping, component, and collaboration skills in the Adobe ecosystem.

PREMIUM

Certified Usability Analyst (CUA, HFI)

~$1,500

Human Factors International certification with a human factors and ergonomics focus. Highly respected in BFSI, enterprise, and healthcare sectors. Validates rigorous research and usability testing capabilities.

FREE

Figma Advanced Certificate

Free on Figma Community

Figma's own advanced certification available through the Figma Community. Proves mastery of components, auto layout, prototyping, and design systems. Practical and respected among design teams using Figma daily.

Skill Milestones by Programme Phase

🌱

Foundational — Weeks 1–4

  • Conduct at least 3 user interviews on a real product
  • Create a sitemap and user flow for a chosen app
  • Build lo-fi wireframes for 5+ screens in Figma
  • Complete a full heuristic evaluation of a real Indian app
⚡

Application — Weeks 5–8

  • Full Figma design with component variants and design system
  • Interactive prototype covering a complete user flow
  • Moderated usability test conducted with 3 participants
  • Design iterated based on test findings with documented changes
🚀

Professional — Weeks 9–12

  • Design system documented with tokens, anatomy, and developer handoff
  • Developer handoff spec complete with spacing grid and asset exports
  • Design sprint facilitated (problem to prototype in one week)
  • Accessibility report with WCAG AA compliance across all screens
💼

Job-Ready

  • 2 UX case studies published on Behance and/or Notion (public)
  • Figma portfolio link shared on LinkedIn Featured section
  • 1 certification completed (Google UX Design or IxDF recommended)
  • 1 ADPList mentor session completed — received feedback on portfolio

Your 90-Day Post-Internship Plan

Phase 1
Days 1–30: Polish & Publish
  • Convert your top 2 projects into fully written UX case studies — publish on Behance and back up on Notion
  • Update your LinkedIn profile: headline should say "UI/UX Designer | Figma | User Research" (not "Fresher" or "Student")
  • Add the Behance/portfolio link to LinkedIn's "Featured" section
  • Complete the Google UX Design Certificate module on portfolio building (Module 6)
  • Ask your mentor or a senior designer to review your case studies before publishing
Phase 2
Days 31–60: Apply & Practice
  • Apply to junior UI/UX roles on LinkedIn (use "Easy Apply" + personalised message) and Naukri — aim for 5 applications per day
  • Practice a 30-minute whiteboard design challenge daily: "Design an app for X" — set a timer, sketch on paper, explain your decisions
  • Attend free ADPList mentoring sessions — book 2 sessions with designers at companies you want to work at
  • Join Figma Community forums and Discord — participate, ask questions, share your work
  • Do 1 freelance project (even unpaid for a local NGO or small business) to add real-world work to your portfolio
Phase 3
Days 61–90: Interview & Negotiate
  • Prepare a 5-minute portfolio walkthrough — always start with the problem, not the screens: "I worked on X because users were struggling with Y"
  • Practice explaining your research decisions with data: "We interviewed 5 users and found that 4 of them couldn't complete checkout because..."
  • Research each company's design maturity before interviewing — look at their product, find their design team on LinkedIn, read their design blog if they have one
  • Negotiate: know your market rate (use LinkedIn Salary, Glassdoor, AmbitionBox) — junior UI/UX roles in Bangalore/Mumbai/Hyderabad pay ₹4–8 LPA for freshers with strong portfolios
  • Follow up within 48 hours after every interview with a brief thank-you note

Where to Apply

Product Companies (Design-Mature)

Razorpay · Groww · Zerodha · CRED · Meesho · Nykaa · PhonePe

Strong design culture with dedicated design teams, design systems, and research operations. Highest growth for UX careers. Competitive pay. Expect portfolio review + design challenge + case study presentation in interviews.

Design Agencies

Elephant Design · Ray+Keshavan (WPP) · Designit India · ThoughtWorks Design

Exposure to diverse clients and project types — from FMCG packaging to SaaS products to government portals. Excellent for skill-building in the first 2 years. Portfolio grows fast. Hours can be long but learning is dense.

IT Services

TCS iON Design Studio · Infosys Digital Experience · Wipro Digital CX · Accenture Song

Volume hiring, structured onboarding, and formal career progression paths. Large design teams mean mentorship opportunities. Certifications are valued and often subsidised. Strong option if you want job security alongside skill development.

MNC India Offices

Google India Design (Bangalore) · Microsoft India UX (Hyderabad) · Amazon India Design · Flipkart Design Hub

Highest standards, best pay, and globally recognised on a resume. Competition is intense — expect multiple portfolio reviews, design exercises, and design critique sessions. Invest 3–6 months in portfolio polish before applying.
