Beginner Level — 1 Month (4 Weeks)

Digital Marketing Basics · SEO · Social Media · Email Marketing · GA4 Setup

Beginner
Learning Objectives
  • Understand the digital marketing funnel and buyer persona creation
  • Perform on-page SEO and keyword research for a website
  • Create a 30-day social media content calendar
  • Set up and send an email campaign using Mailchimp
  • Configure Google Analytics 4 and read core metrics
  • Write a competitor analysis report
Weekly Curriculum
WeekTopicsLabAssignment
Week 1 Digital marketing ecosystem overview; TOFU/MOFU/BOFU funnel; buyer personas (demographics, goals, pain points, channels); owned vs earned vs paid media; setting up Google Analytics 4 + Search Console; Google Tag Manager basics Audit a real website's current digital marketing presence using GA4 + Search Console Competitor analysis for 3 brands (traffic, keywords, social, content strategy)
Week 2 On-page SEO: title tags (60 chars), meta descriptions (160 chars), H1-H6 hierarchy, alt text, internal linking, keyword density; Keyword research (search volume, KD, CPC, intent: informational/transactional/commercial/navigational); Google Keyword Planner + Ubersuggest; technical SEO basics (site speed, mobile-friendliness, XML sitemap, robots.txt) On-page optimization of 5 existing blog posts for target keywords Keyword map (50 keywords) for a niche website with intent classification
Week 3 Platform strategy (Instagram, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube, Pinterest — when to use each); content types (educational, entertaining, promotional, community); Canva for social media graphics; content calendar structure; posting consistency; hashtag strategy; engagement tactics Create 10 social media posts across 3 platforms for a fictional brand 30-day content calendar with captions, hashtags, optimal posting times per platform
Week 4 Mailchimp setup: account, audience lists, signup forms; email anatomy (subject line, preheader, header, body, CTA, footer); welcome sequence design; A/B testing subject lines; open rate, CTR, unsubscribe rate; GA4: users, sessions, bounce rate, engagement rate, conversion events Create and send 3-email welcome sequence in Mailchimp GA4 performance report + email campaign analysis with recommendations

Sample Viva Questions

Beginner Viva

  • What is the difference between SEO and SEM?
  • Name 3 factors that affect a website's ranking on Google.
  • What is a buyer persona and why create one?
  • What does CTR stand for and how is it calculated?

Intermediate Viva

  • Explain Quality Score in Google Ads and its components.
  • What is retargeting and how does it work technically?
  • How does last-touch attribution differ from linear attribution?
  • What KPIs would you track for an e-commerce campaign?

Advanced Viva

  • How do you calculate marketing ROI and Customer Lifetime Value?
  • Design a growth experiment to increase free trial conversions by 20%.
  • What is the AARRR framework and how would you apply it to a SaaS product?
  • How would you allocate a ₹10 lakh monthly marketing budget across channels?
