Beginner Level — 1 Month (4 Weeks)

Design Theory · Color & Typography · Figma Basics · Wireframing · Clickable Prototype

Beginner
Learning Objectives
  • Apply color theory, typography, and Gestalt principles to UI design
  • Create components, styles, and auto layouts in Figma
  • Build low-fidelity wireframes for a mobile application
  • Map user flows and information architecture
  • Create an interactive clickable prototype in Figma
  • Design social media graphics with Adobe Photoshop
Weekly Curriculum
WeekTopicsHands-on LabAssignment
Week 1 Color theory (hue, saturation, value, complementary/analogous/triadic palettes, contrast accessibility); Typography (scale, hierarchy, font pairing, leading, tracking, readability); Gestalt principles (proximity, alignment, similarity, contrast, figure-ground); Grid systems and whitespace; Design for screens (72 DPI vs 300 DPI) Create a 5-color brand palette + typographic poster using Figma Brand palette for a fictional startup + redesign a cluttered webpage header
Week 2 Figma interface: frames, layers, groups, components, styles; Auto Layout (padding, gap, alignment, fill/fixed); Constraints and responsive resizing; Color and text styles; Component properties and variants; Vector editing tools, boolean operations, pen tool Button component (5 variants: default/hover/active/disabled/loading) + icon set Form field component library with error states + 8-icon custom set
Week 3 User flows and information architecture (card sorting, tree testing); Lo-fi wireframing methodology; Paper prototyping concepts; Content hierarchy: F-pattern, Z-pattern, visual scanning; Mobile-first design patterns (thumb zones, safe areas) Map 5-screen checkout user flow + wireframe a food delivery app (8 screens) Complete wireframe set for a 10-screen mobile app of your choice
Week 4 Figma interactive prototyping: connections, transitions (Smart Animate, dissolve, move in/out), overlays, scrolling; Photoshop basics (layers, masks, blending modes, smart objects, retouching); Presenting and sharing designs (Figma share link, Loom walkthrough) Clickable food delivery prototype with 5 interactive flows 3 Instagram post templates in Photoshop + Figma prototype presentation video

WeekTopicsLabAssignment

Sample Viva Questions

Beginner Viva

  • What is the difference between UI and UX?
  • Explain 3 Gestalt principles with examples.
  • Why is color contrast important in accessibility?
  • What is Auto Layout in Figma and why use it?

Intermediate Viva

  • How do you validate a design decision with data?
  • What is the difference between a lo-fi and hi-fi wireframe?
  • Explain the atomic design methodology.
  • How do you conduct a usability test? Walk me through your process.

Advanced Viva

  • How do you ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance in a design system?
  • Walk me through a design decision you made that you had to defend to stakeholders.
  • How do you measure the success of a UX redesign?
  • What is the purpose of a design sprint and when would you use one?
