Program Duration Options

1 Month

Intensive foundational program for summer/winter short internships.

  • 4 weeks structured curriculum
  • 30–35 hrs/week
  • 1 mini project
  • Beginner level certification
  • Ideal for Diploma & 1st year students

3 Months

Semester internship for intermediate skill development and project building.

  • 12 weeks structured curriculum
  • 25–30 hrs/week
  • 2 projects (mini + capstone)
  • Intermediate certification
  • Ideal for BCA/B.Tech 3rd year

6 Months

Full industrial training for advanced, job-ready skill development.

  • 24 weeks structured curriculum
  • 20–25 hrs/week
  • 3 projects + enterprise capstone
  • Advanced certification + placement
  • Ideal for MCA/M.Tech final year
